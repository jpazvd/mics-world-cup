# Social posts — The MICS World Cup '26

Draft announcement copy for sharing the project. Not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA;
"World Cup" is used as a metaphor.

## LinkedIn

⚽ What if the World Cup were won not on goals — but on how well a country measures the lives of its children?

I replayed the official 2026 FIFA World Cup draw — all 48 nations, the real groups — but every match is decided by each country's **MICS survey-programme strength**: how deep, how current and how continuous its Multiple Indicator Cluster Surveys are (427 survey records across MICS1–MICS7).

Fully reproducible and deterministic (seed = 1995, the year MICS began). 🏆 Champion: Iraq.

It's a communication device, not a data-quality ranking — a playful way to celebrate the countries investing in measuring children's health, nutrition, education and protection. You can't improve what you don't measure.

Open source (code, data, figures): https://github.com/jpazvd/mics-world-cup
